WHAT IS BLOCKCHAIN TECHNOLOGY ?

A blockchain platform is a digital ledger that allows users to record transactions across multiple computers or nodes. It is designed to be transparent, secure, and tamper-proof, making it an innovative solution for managung and verifying digital assets.

At it’s core, a blockchain is a chain of block, with each containing a list of transaction and each block is linked to previous block through a cryptographic hash, forming a continuous chain.

HOW BLOCKCHAIN TECHNOLOGY CONCEPT WILL IMPACT MALAYSIA’S EDUCATION SECTOR?

Here are several ways blockchain can influence and enhance education in Malaysia:

1. Efficient Data Management:

Blockchain can dacilitate efficient and secure management of student data. With blockchain-based systems, students can maintain ownership and control over their personal data, deciding who can access and utilize it.

2. Transparent & Verifiable Qualifications:

Blockchain can be used to securely store and verify the certification and qualifications of academic.

3. Streamlined International Student Mobility:

students can share their educationl records with institution abroad and enabling efficient recognition of prior learning.
